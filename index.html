ِAbout the show

Set in 1980s Los Angeles, Snowfall chronicles the rise of the crack cocaine epidemic and the lives it changes.
The show explores crime, ambition, and fate as different worlds collide.

The gripping narrative follows Franklin Saint, a young street entrepreneur, as he’s thrust into a dangerous underworld.
Power, family ties, and moral choices drive the story forward.

Snowfall’s sharp storytelling and atmospheric visuals capture a pivotal era,
earning acclaim for its performances and realism.
